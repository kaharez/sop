# LXIII. NATIONAL EDUCATION & PUBLIC LEARNING DEFINITION

## 1. Purpose
To promote understanding of the Principality’s identity, values, heritage, and
governance among citizens and the wider world.

## 2. Educational Functions

### A. Public Learning Initiatives
Articles, exhibitions, publications, digital resources.

### B. Instructional Materials
Guides on governance, history, identity, ceremonial tradition, and legal
structures.

### C. Collaboration

## Partnerships with
- schools,
- universities,
- museums,
- cultural institutions.

## 3. Standards of Educational Content

## Educational materials must be
- accurate,
- accessible,
- respectful of national identity,
- non‑partisan.

## 4. Programme governance
- Educational outputs are reviewed for factual accuracy, cultural integrity,
  and alignment with national identity; approvals are logged.
- Partnerships with institutions are formalised through agreements registered
  by the Royal Chancellery; outcomes and learnings are reported.

## 5. Measurement and outreach
- Track participation, feedback, and impact of public learning initiatives to
  improve content and reach.
- Use Government Communications channels to promote resources; ensure materials
  comply with accessibility standards and are available in digital formats.
