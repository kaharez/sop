# XXXVI. EXPANDED SECTION: DOCUMENTARY TEMPLATES (FORMAL INSTRUMENTS)

This section provides the official structures and required components for all
formal documents issued by or under the authority of the Sovereign. These
templates standardise presentation, ensure legal clarity, and preserve the
dignity of state instruments.

### A. Template: ROYAL DECREE
```text
[Heading]
PRINCIPALITY OF KAHARAGIA
ROYAL DECREE
Number: [Year/Sequential Number]
Date: [Day Month Year]

[Authority Clause]
By the authority vested in the Sovereign of the Principality of Kaharagia, and
in accordance with the constitutional and administrative order of the State,
the following is decreed:

[Decree Text]
SECTION 1. [Title of Section]
Provide clear, declarative text establishing the rule, institution, appointment,
or policy.

SECTION 2. [Further Provisions]
Detail any additional measures, authorities, or instructions.

[Execution Clause]
This Decree shall take effect immediately upon its publication in the Royal
Gazette. The Royal Chancellery is directed to register, seal, and promulgate
this instrument.

[Signature Block]
Signed:
[Sovereign’s Name], Sovereign of Kaharagia

Authenticated by:
Clerk of the Royal Chancellery
Great Seal affixed.
```

### B. Template: LETTER PATENT
```text
[Heading]
PRINCIPALITY OF KAHARAGIA
LETTER PATENT

[Opening Formula]
To all persons to whom these presents shall come, greetings.

[Purpose]
Know that We, in the exercise of Our Sovereign authority, do by these Letters
Patent confer, establish, or appoint the following:

[Body]
- Grant of honour or dignity
- Creation of office or institution
- Confirmation of rights or privileges

[Legal Confirmation]
These Letters Patent are issued under Our hand and the Great Seal, and shall
be entered into the Register of Letters Patent by the Royal Chancellery.

[Signature Block]
Executed at [location or digital equivalent] this [date].
[Sovereign’s Name], Sovereign of Kaharagia.
Clerk of the Royal Chancellery.
```

### C. Template: ROYAL WARRANT
```text
[Heading]
ROYAL WARRANT
Issued by the Sovereign of Kaharagia

[Purpose]
A warrant authorising an individual or body to perform a specific act,
hold an office, or receive recognition (e.g., purveyors, appointments).

[Body]
- Name of the recipient
- Nature of authority granted
- Conditions or limitations, if any

[Signature Block]
Signed and sealed this [date].
Sovereign of Kaharagia
Countersigned: Clerk of the Royal Chancellery
```

### D. Template: GAZETTE NOTICE
```text
[Heading]
ROYAL GAZETTE
Official Notice

[Body]
- Title of notice
- Issuing authority
- Text of notice, formatted clearly

[Authentication]
Published by the Office of Government Communications
Authenticated by the Royal Chancellery
Reference Number: [XXXX]
```

### E. Template: DIPLOMATIC NOTE
```text
[Heading]
NOTE VERBALE
Principality of Kaharagia
Office of Foreign Affairs & Diplomatic Service

[Body]
The Office presents its compliments to the [Embassy/Office] of [Country] and
has the honour to communicate the following:

[Message Content]

The Office avails itself of this opportunity to renew the assurances of its
highest consideration.

[Signature Block]
Office of Foreign Affairs
Principality of Kaharagia
Date: [Day Month Year]
```

### F. Template: ADMINISTRATIVE NOTICE
```text
[Heading]
ROYAL GAZETTE
Administrative Notice

[Body]
- Title of notice
- Issuing Office
- Authority reference (e.g., Royal Decree No. [XX]/[YYYY])
- Effective date and scope

[Authentication]
Published by the Office of Government Communications
Reference Number: [XXXX]
```

- **Usage notes:**
- All templates must be completed with correct numbering, authority clauses,
  and dates before submission to the Royal Chancellery.
- Digital versions must include approved signatures/hashes; physical versions
  must be sealed where prescribed.
