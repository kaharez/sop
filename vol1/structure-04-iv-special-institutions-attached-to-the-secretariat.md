# IV. SPECIAL INSTITUTIONS ATTACHED TO THE SECRETARIAT

## 1. Royal Chancellery
   Leadership: Clerk of the Royal Chancellery (Deputy Secretary of State)
   Roles:
   - Prepares, authenticates, and seals Royal Decrees, Letters Patent,
     Charters, and Warrants.
   - Keeper of the Great Seal.
   - Issues documents jointly with the Office of Government Communications
     for publication in the Royal Gazette.
   - Maintains the state archives of formal instruments.
   - Oversees the legislative assent process.

## 2. Chancellery of the Kaharagian Honours
   Leadership: Secretary of the Honours Chancellery
   Roles:
   - Administers Orders, Decorations, and Medals.
   - Maintains the Register of Honours.
   - Organises investitures and honours ceremonies.
   - Designs insignia with the Institute of Heraldry.
   - Advises the Sovereign on honours policy.

## 3. Institute of Heraldry
   Leadership: Chief Herald of Kaharagia
   Roles:
   - Grants and registers coats of arms, flags, badges.
   - Creates and maintains the Register of Arms, Flags, and Badges.
   - Designs state symbols, seals, banners, and heraldic devices.
   - Advises on identity, symbolism, and ceremonial representation.
   - Ensures heraldic law and cultural continuity.
