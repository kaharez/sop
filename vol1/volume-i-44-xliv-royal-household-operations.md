## 3. Purveyors to the Court

### A. Appointment
Purveyors are appointed by Royal Warrant.

### B. Standards and Obligations
- Maintain excellence, reliability, and ethical conduct.
- Adhere to protocol, procurement standards, and ethical sourcing.
- Align with state values and quality expectations.

### C. Oversight and Loss of Appointment
The Royal Household reviews appointments annually and may withdraw a warrant
for declining standards, ethical breaches, or reputational harm.

## 4. Internal Conduct Rules
Household staff must maintain confidentiality, act with discretion, observe
decorum, and ensure professionalism in all interactions.

## 5. Records and coordination
- Maintains inventories of regalia, hospitality assets, and purveyor services;
  reconciles usage with protocol and budget records.
- Coordinates with Government Communications and the Royal Chancellery for
  formal invitations and ceremonial documentation handled by the Household.

## 6. Security and access
- Enforces visitor screening and access controls within Royal Household spaces.
- Works with Defence Policy & National Security and Digital Government on
  physical and cyber safeguards for household operations and data.
