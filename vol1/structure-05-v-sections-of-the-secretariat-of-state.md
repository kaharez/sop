# V. SECTIONS OF THE SECRETARIAT OF STATE

SECTION I:
SECRETARIAT OF STATE — SECTION FOR GENERAL AFFAIRS & NATIONAL ADMINISTRATION
Leadership: Undersecretary of State for General Affairs

A. Office of Government Communications & Public Information
   - Publishes the Royal Gazette (with the Chancellery).
   - Coordinates official statements and announcements.
   - Oversees branding, design system compliance, public channels.
   - Ensures transparency and public access to state information.

B. Office of Citizenship, Identity & Naturalisation
   - Manages the National Register of Citizens.
   - Issues National Identification Numbers (NINs).
   - Handles naturalisation, renunciation, documentation.
   - Oversees identity documents and digital identity (in cooperation
     with Digital Government).

C. Office of Laws & Justice
   - Provides legal counsel to the Sovereign and Secretariat.
   - Drafts legislation, codification, constitutional review.
   - Oversees publication of laws and legal archives.
   - Ensures legal compliance of institutions.

D. Office of State Ceremonies & Protocol
   - Manages state ceremonies, national events, diplomatic protocol.
   - Coordinates precedence, ceremonial orders, and investitures.
   - Works with the Royal Court and the Honours Chancellery.

SECTION II:
SECRETARIAT OF STATE — SECTION FOR EXTERNAL RELATIONS & INTERNATIONAL PRESENCE
Leadership: Undersecretary of State for External Relations

A. Office of Foreign Affairs & Diplomatic Service
   - Maintains diplomatic missions (even if honorary).
   - Represents Kaharagia internationally.
   - Negotiates treaties (approved by Sovereign).
   - Provides consular-style services to nationals abroad.
   - Builds international partnerships.

B. Office of Defence Policy & National Security
   - Develops national security strategy.
   - Focuses on cyber-defence and sovereignty of digital systems.
   - Coordinates emergency planning and intelligence awareness.
   - Liaises with future military structures under the Sovereign.

SECTION III:
SECRETARIAT OF STATE — SECTION FOR CULTURAL, ACADEMIC & DIGITAL NATION-BUILDING
Leadership: Undersecretary of State for Culture & Digital Development

A. Office of Culture, Language & Heritage
   - Promotes the Kaharagian language and cultural identity.
   - Oversees museums, archives, heritage preservation.
   - Coordinates national cultural programs and symbolic projects.
   - Works closely with the Institute of Heraldry.

B. Office of Education, Science & Innovation
   - Develops national curricula, partnerships with educational bodies.
   - Oversees academic grants, research initiatives.
   - Supports digital learning platforms such as PastPortal.
   - Encourages scientific progress among nationals.

C. Office of Digital Government & Cybersecurity
   - Manages ePortal and digital infrastructure.
   - Oversees secure digital identity.
   - Protects government information systems.
   - Develops national digital sovereignty strategies.
