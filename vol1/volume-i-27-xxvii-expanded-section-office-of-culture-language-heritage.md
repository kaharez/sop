# XXVII. EXPANDED SECTION: OFFICE OF CULTURE, LANGUAGE & HERITAGE

### 1. Purpose
This Office preserves national identity and ensures the continuity of
Kaharagiaâ€™s cultural heritage, historical memory, and educational symbols.

### 2. Responsibilities

#### A. Cultural Programmes
- Supports national traditions,
- develops cultural initiatives,
- coordinates with museums and cultural institutions.

#### B. Heritage Protection
- Identifies cultural assets,
- maintains historical archives (in coordination with Chancellery),
- oversees preservation initiatives.

#### C. National Identity
- Promotes symbols and traditions,
- ensures accuracy in cultural representation,
- maintains documentation relating to national heritage.

### 3. Research and Publications

### Prepares
- educational materials,
- reports on cultural policy,
- heritage guidelines.

### 4. Archives and preservation
- Coordinates with the Royal Chancellery and National Archives to catalogue and
  safeguard cultural artefacts, recordings, and ceremonial records.
- Oversees digitisation priorities to ensure public access where appropriate.

### 5. Programmes and recognition
- Designs cultural observances, awards, and exhibitions to strengthen national
  identity and public engagement.
- Publishes cultural notices and programme details in the Gazette for
  transparency and archival completeness.
