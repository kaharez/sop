# III. THE SECRETARIAT OF STATE

The central governing body of Kaharagia, equivalent to a ministry-level body.
It coordinates all governmental functions, both internal and external.

#### A. Secretary of State
- Head of Government; primary advisor to the Sovereign.
- Oversees all Sections, Offices, and institutions.
- Ensures execution of laws, decrees, and national policy.

#### B. Assistant Secretary of State
- Manages day-to-day functioning.
- Ensures administrative continuity.
- Coordinates inter-office cooperation.
